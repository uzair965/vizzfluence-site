My 5-step process

Clear steps. Fast turnarounds. Transparent scores and rationales you can share with stakeholders.

1. Brief & ICP

Define goals, audience, channels, geos, budget, and must-haves.

2. Longlist

Generate a wide set across platforms. De-dupe, tag by niche and format.

3. Vetting

Fraud checks, growth curves, audience geos, brand safety, sponsors, rate sanity.

4. Shortlist

Rank by fit score with notes & watchouts. Present 10–30 creators.

5. Outreach

Personalized emails/DMs, follow-ups, negotiation, and handoff.
